About Me


Richie Yin


A passionate software engineer with a strong background in leadership and a lifelong love for technology. Having been in leadership roles since grade school, I've honed my management skills through diverse experiences such as leading projects, running a restaurant, overseeing a sales team, and managing a business.

Over the recent months, I have chosen to deepen my understanding and expand my technical skill set through an immersive coding bootcamp. This rigorous self-investment has allowed me to master a wide array of programming languages and frameworks. In doing so, I have significantly enhanced my software engineering capabilities, enabling me to tackle increasingly complex technical challenges with greater confidence.
